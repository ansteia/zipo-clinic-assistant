## 2. What Personal Information We Collect

### Identifying Information
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Address
- Phone number
- Email address
- Medicare details
- Health fund details

### Health Information
- Medical history
- Test results
- Referrals
- Prescriptions
- Consultation notes
- Treatment plans

Health information is considered sensitive information under Australian law and receives additional protections.

### Information Collected Through the Virtual Assistant

If you use the Zipo virtual assistant to request a call or report a booking issue, we may collect:
- Your phone number
- Your email address (if provided)
- A brief description of your request

We do **not** request detailed medical history, attachments, Medicare numbers, or diagnostic documents through the chatbot.

---

## 3. Why We Collect Your Information

We collect personal information for purposes including:
- Providing medical services
- Arranging appointments
- Issuing prescriptions
- Preparing referrals
- Conducting telephone consultations
- Responding to enquiries
- Meeting legal and regulatory obligations

Information submitted through the chatbot is used solely to:
- Arrange a return call
- Resolve booking-related issues
- Respond to non-urgent enquiries

---

## 4. Consent

We collect sensitive information only when:
- You provide informed consent, and
- It is reasonably necessary to provide healthcare services.

For the virtual assistant service:
- You must provide explicit consent before submitting personal contact details.
- Information is collected only for the specific purpose stated at the time of collection.

---

## 5. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

This includes:
- Secure electronic medical record systems
- Password-protected access controls
- Encrypted website connections (HTTPS)
- Secure email systems
- Limited staff access on a need-to-know basis

The virtual assistant system operates through secure hosting providers. Information submitted is transmitted securely and is **not** used for artificial intelligence model training.

---

## 6. Use of Third-Party Service Providers

We may use third-party providers to support our services, including:
- Online booking platforms (e.g., HotDoc)
- Secure website hosting providers
- Email systems
- Virtual assistant platforms

These providers may process information solely for the purpose of delivering services on our behalf. They are not authorised to use patient data for independent purposes.

---

## 7. Data Retention

Medical records are retained in accordance with Australian healthcare record-keeping laws.

Information submitted through the chatbot for callback requests is retained only as necessary to:
- Process the request
- Conduct the associated consultation
- Comply with medical record obligations (where applicable)

## 8. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose personal information:
- To other healthcare providers involved in your care
- To specialists (for referrals)
- To pathology or diagnostic services
- To Medicare or health insurers
- Where required by law

We do **not** sell personal information.
