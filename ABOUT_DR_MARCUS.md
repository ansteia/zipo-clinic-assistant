# About Dr Marcus Coelho

## Who Is Dr Marcus?

Dr Marcus Coelho is a General Practitioner at **Kiama Downs Medical Practice** (31 Johnson Street, Kiama Downs NSW 2533). He joined the practice in **December 2024**.

Originally from **Brazil**, Dr Marcus brings a unique international background and a deep commitment to holistic, patient-centred care.

---

## Qualifications & Training

- **Specialist degree in Intensive Care Medicine** (2010)
- **Diploma and Fellowship in Point of Care Ultrasonography (POCUS)**
- **Instructor with the Australian College of Emergency Medicine** — trains ultrasonography professionals

---

## Professional Background

Dr Marcus has an extensive and diverse clinical background spanning three countries and multiple specialties:

- Established and ran an **Intensive Care Unit in the Brazilian Amazon** (until 2016)
- Worked in **Intensive Care Medicine**, **Emergency Medicine**, and **General Practice**
- Transitioned to General Practice to focus on long-term, holistic patient care
- Holds a fellowship in **Point of Care Ultrasonography** — an advanced skill rarely found in GP settings

---

## Clinical Specialties & Interests

- Complex medical needs management
- Metabolic syndromes
- Congestive heart failure / Heart failure management
- Palliative care
- Point of Care Ultrasonography (POCUS)
- Urgent and emergency presentations

---

## Philosophy

Dr Marcus believes in treating the whole person, not just the condition. His approach combines the precision of emergency and intensive care medicine with the continuity and warmth of general practice. He is passionate about helping patients with complex needs navigate their health with clarity and confidence.
